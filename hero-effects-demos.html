Hero Image Effects

Post-reveal animations to bring the hero to life

1. Slow Ken Burns

Cinematic
A subtle, continuous zoom and pan that gives the image a cinematic, living quality. Slowly scales from 100% to 108% over 20 seconds.

2. Parallax Mouse Tracking

Interactive
The image subtly shifts based on mouse position, creating depth and interactivity. Move your mouse around the image to see the effect.

3. Reactive Light Flares

Stage Lighting
Soft glows that follow the mouse cursor, plus a gentle pulsing flare. Like a follow-spot responding to movement — ties into the lighting theme.

4. Drifting Color Wash

Stage Gels
A slow-moving gradient overlay that shifts through colors like stage lighting gels. Subtle enough to not distract but adds atmosphere.

5. Floating Haze Particles

Atmospheric
Small, semi-transparent particles drift across the image like dust in a light beam or theatrical haze. Connects to the geometric particle network.
