Hero Image Effects v2

Alternative approaches to bring the hero to life

1. Image Crossfade

Slideshow
Multiple photos slowly crossfade between each other. Creates variety without being distracting. Requires 2-4 different photos.
⚠️ Would need multiple hero-worthy photos

2. Animated Film Grain

Cinematic
A subtle, organic film grain overlay that gives the image texture and a cinematic, analog feel. Adds life without altering composition.

3. Double Exposure Blend

Artistic
A second image (light beams) subtly blends with the portrait using screen blend mode. Creates depth and ties into the lighting theme.

4. Hover/Scroll Transform

Interactive
Image starts grayscale and transforms to color on hover (or scroll on the real page). Creates a reveal moment as users engage.
Hover to reveal color

5. Vignette Breathing

Subtle
A gentle pulsing vignette that expands and contracts, creating a "breathing" focus effect. Very subtle — doesn't move the image.

6. Subtle Glitch

Edgy
RGB split and digital distortion on hover. Gives an edgy, tech-forward vibe. Hover over the image to see the effect.
