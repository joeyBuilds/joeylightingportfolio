Hero Reveal Options

Click "Replay" to see each animation again

1. Spotlight Reveal

Stage Lighting
A circular spotlight expands from center, as if a follow-spot is illuminating the subject. Includes a soft bloom glow that fades as the image is revealed.

2. Light Beam Wipe

Scanner Effect
A bright beam of light sweeps horizontally across the screen, revealing the image as it passes. Creates a dramatic scanner/moving head effect.

3. Vertical Shutter Reveal

Theatrical
Vertical slices open from the center outward, matching the vertical bars in the hero image. Creates a dramatic curtain-parting or light-rig reveal effect.

4. Particle Materialization

Ethereal
The image emerges from a bright, blurred state with particles converging — as if materializing from light and energy. Ties into the existing geometric network aesthetic.

5. Cinematic Letterbox

Movie Premiere
Black bars retract from top and bottom while the image scales in with a fading vignette. Creates a widescreen, film-premiere moment.
